The Serio Verify
suite

We have divided our services into three different products. You can purchase them individually or as the combined Serio Verify suite.

Neural

Predictive lead scoring that tells you who to go for and who to avoid. It is machine learning for your marketing and sales.

The Verify part

Enrich your leads in real time with over 200 demographic variables as they sign up.

The Neural part

Match them to your customized machine learning model and find out the likelihood of purchase and which product your lead is interested in before contacting them.

Connect

The perfect self-service platform for small and medium sized companies that work with leads.

The Verify part

Validate and enrich your leads in real time as the sign up or by uploading batches.

The Connect part

Connect and integrate with tons of out-of-box integrations to email systems, dialers and many other marketing and sales tools. If it is not built in, use our generic integration tool or have Serio Verify make you a custom integration in no time.

Optimize

Our flagship product for enterprise companies looking to optimizes their lead-to-sale journey. Comes with full access to our team of experienced consultants.

The Verify part

Validate and enrich your leads in real time as the sign up or by uploading batches.

The Optimize part

Get real time KPIs in your dashboard, set up automized trigger-based flows, ensure capacity control of multiple call centers and insights into the entire lead-to-sale value chain.
